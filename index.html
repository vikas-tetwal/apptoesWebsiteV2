Hiring Models for Software Development Projects

we offer flexible hiring models tailored to your project needs.

Dedicated Developer Hiring

you can hire a single developer to contribute directly to your software development project. Whether you need a programmer, designer, or any other specialist, your dedicated developer will function just like your own remote employee—fully focused on your goals. You maintain complete control over tasks and workflows without worrying about workspace, hardware, software, office infrastructure, HR, or supervision. We handle all of that for you, allowing you to stay focused on your project and business growth.

Read More

Agile Hiring with Full Control

You assemble a flexible team of dedicated and shared professionals—including developers, designers, analysts, software architects, testers, and DevOps engineers—to build your software application. Since not all requirements can be defined upfront, the project follows agile methodologies, allowing for iterative development and continuous refinement. Throughout the process, you maintain control over the team, timeline, and budget while closely monitoring progress. This ensures the final product aligns with your vision and delivers maximum value.

Read More

Project-Based Hiring

With Assignment-Based Hiring, you partner with us to execute your project rather than hiring individual developers. Pricing is based on the assignment's scope, defined by clear requirements and quality benchmarks. Costs remain fixed unless the scope changes, ensuring transparency and predictability. A project can include multiple assignments, and you can seamlessly combine dedicated and assignment-based approaches to optimize efficiency and flexibility.
